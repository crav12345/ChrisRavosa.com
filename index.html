About

Hello! I'm Christopher, a software developer specializing in video game development. Some of my earliest memories are of playing Legend of Zelda and Mario Party with my older brothers on our cousin's N64. Decades later, we still stay close by playing games with each other online. Needless to say, games have been a big part of my life. So much so, that I've made a career out of crafting meaningful gaming experiences for players!

I prepared for my career in game development by getting my bachelor's degree in computer science and games & emerging media from Marist College. After completing my undergraduate studies, I stayed at Marist for a fifth year to complete my master's degree in software development.

My game development career has taken me to a location-based gaming startup, two Activision development studios, and most recently Naughty Dog. In addition to my professional career, I spend time developing my own indie games, bots, mobile applications, and other fun software. While you're here, please take a look at some of my work. Thanks for visiting!

- Chris Ravosa

Call of Duty: Vanguard

Call of Duty: Vanguard is a 2021 first-person shooter game published by Activision, whose development was led by Sledgehammer Games. The levels Christopher primarily contributed to include Operation Tonga, Numa Numa Trail, and The Fourth Reich.
