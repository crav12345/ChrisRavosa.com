About Me

Hi! I'm Christopher Ravosa, a passionate software engineer and game developer with a focus on creating immersive and engaging gaming experiences. With several years of experience in the industry, I specialize in writing clean, efficient code for both game mechanics and developer tools.

My Projects

Here are some of the exciting projects I've worked on throughout my career as a game developer:

MLB on Apple Vision Pro

Leveraged Apple's groundbreaking hardware and VisionOS operating system to contribute to the creation of the most immersive viewing experience available for baseball.

Home Run Derby Mobile

Maintained and expanded upon the systems that drive a popular mobile game. These include gameplay systems written in C#, Rest APIs written in Typescript, C# matchmaking servers, NoSQL databases, and more.

The Last of Us Online

Co-owned the design and implementation of gameplay interactables and the scavenging experience for a large-scale looter shooter. Contributed to the development of networked scripting paradigms and the implementation of those experimental paradigms across a variety of gameplay systems.

Call of Duty: Vanguard

Designed and implemented dogfights and visual FX on the final mission of the campaign. Scripted audio and animation events across multiple levels. Contributed to the design and implementation of gameplay progression on the second and third levels of the game.
